The Architecture of Movement and Waves

To watch the swell rise from deep ocean trenches is to witness the physical unfolding of memory. Water remembers the wind that generated it thousands of miles away, carrying its signature across the globe to break upon our sands.

In this dynamic interface between water, salt, and morning sky, time ceases to act as an arrow. Instead, it mirrors the tides—recursive, deep, and beautifully indifferent to human structures.
